About Rebels Kickboxing

Rebels Kickboxing was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate martial artists with a common goal: to share their love for kickboxing and create a community where people of all ages and skill levels could train together. We take pride in our knowledgeable instructors, modern facilities, and diverse classes tailored to accommodate different fitness goals.

Our Philosophy

At Rebels Kickboxing, we believe in empowering our students through the practice of martial arts. Our classes focus not only on physical fitness and technique but also on building self-confidence, discipline, and perseverance. We are dedicated to providing a positive, supportive environment where everyone can grow and achieve their personal best.

Our Instructors

Our team of highly skilled instructors brings a wealth of experience and expertise to our gym. They come from diverse backgrounds in martial arts, including kickboxing, Muay Thai, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Our instructors are dedicated to helping each student reach their full potential and develop the skills they need to succeed both inside and outside the gym.

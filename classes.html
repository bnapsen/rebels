Classes

We offer a variety of classes to accommodate all skill levels and interests. Browse our class descriptions below to find the perfect fit for you.

Beginner Kickboxing

This class is designed for those who are new to kickboxing or looking to brush up on the fundamentals. You'll learn basic techniques, proper form, and basic combinations. This class provides a great workout while building a strong foundation for future progress.

Intermediate Kickboxing

Intermediate Kickboxing is perfect for students who have mastered the basics and are looking to take their skills to the next level. In this class, you'll learn more advanced combinations, techniques, and strategies while improving your overall fitness and conditioning.

Advanced Kickboxing

Our Advanced Kickboxing class is designed for experienced students who want to refine their skills and challenge themselves. This class focuses on advanced techniques, complex combinations, and high-intensity workouts. Sparring is optional but encouraged for those who want to put their skills to the test.

Kids Kickboxing

Our Kids Kickboxing program is designed to introduce children to the world of martial arts in a fun and safe environment. Classes focus on basic techniques, discipline, and respect, while promoting physical fitness and building self-confidence. Suitable for ages 6-12.

Women's Kickboxing

Our Women's Kickboxing class offers a fun, supportive environment for women to learn kickboxing and improve their fitness. Classes focus on technique, self-defense, and conditioning. All skill levels are welcome.
